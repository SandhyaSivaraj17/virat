Virat Kolhi

Kohli was a key member of the Indian team that won the 2011 Cricket World Cup, 2013 Champions Trophy and 2024 T20 World Cup and captained India to win the ICC Test mace three consecutive times in 2017, 2018, and 2019.


He representsRoyal Challengers Bengaluru in the Indian Premier League and Delhi in domestic cricket.


Kohli has accumulated an impressive 13,906 runs in 295 ODIs, along with 8,871 runs in Test cricket prior to this innings. In T20Is, he amassed 4,188 runs before announcing his retirement from the shortest format after India's triumphant campaign in the T20 World Cup.
